Pebble Light Strand Spool
=========================

This is a customizable model that makes it easy to create custom spools for
winding up addressable fairy/pebble/seed LED strands and other things of
similar sizes.

The central core is designed to make it easy to hand-wind them, as well as let
them unwind by spinning them on your hand/arm. You can put your finger in one
of the side-holes and spin the spool in your hand to in order to crank it a
little.

I use this with battery-powered WLED controllers to make fun decorative lights
at outdoor events.
